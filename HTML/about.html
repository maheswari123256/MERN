Our Tea Story

From a small stall to a beloved tea destination ☕

How It All Began

Our tea journey started in 1995 with a tiny stall near the bus stand of Madurai. What began as a simple idea to serve fresh, aromatic tea quickly became a local favorite. With love, quality, and passion, we grew from a small shop to a cozy tea house where friends and families gather every day.

Our Journey

1995: The first tea stall was opened by our founder, Mr. Ramesh, with just one kettle and a dream.
2005: Expanded to a bigger shop and introduced a variety of teas – ginger tea, masala tea, and lemon tea.
2015: Became a local hangout spot, famous for our signature "Special Dum Tea".
2022: Started online delivery and catering services for events.

Our Promise

Every cup we serve carries our tradition — fresh ingredients, perfect brew, and the warmth of homemade hospitality. We believe tea is not just a drink; it’s an emotion that connects people.
